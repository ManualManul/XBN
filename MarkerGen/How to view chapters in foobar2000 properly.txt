https://www.foobar2000.org/components/view/foo_input_ffmpeg

1. Install the plugin onto a beta foobar2000
2. Point it at a ffmpeg install folder
3. Add M4A to the list of enabled decoders
4. Bump the MP4 decoder to the bottom so that it's below the ffmpeg decoder.

Warning: seeking and playing specific chapters may be slow with this method.
